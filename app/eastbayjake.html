Ziploop

Lead Software Engineer

Ziploop's web app helps shoppers organize their receipts and rewards by uploading a picture. An OCR process scrapes text so users don't have to fill out fields themselves. Ziploop also gives users a dedicated email address where retailers can send receipts or promotional email — receipts get pulled out and sorted accordingly while promotional emails get displayed in a user-friendly newsfeed format.

Responsibilities:

  • Full-stack feature development
  • Manage kanban/scrum and Git workflow for two team engineers
  • Owner for Cordova/PhoneGap + iOS app, including builds and updates
  • In-house expert for Google Analytics and Angular SEO.

Major Projects:

  • Reduced the app's footprint by 80% from 1.5MB to ~300KB
  • Built Ziploop's first analytics and business intelligence dashboard
  • Built Ziploop's entire push notification pipeline, from user opt-in to distribution
  • Overhauled login/signup to be more user-friendly, including Facebook Login integration
  • Built retailer-facing dashboard for distributing rewards and promotions to users
  • Built a real-time map to display signups and upload activity by location
  • Refactored our Capture Image functionality to be 75% smaller and eliminate jQuery dependence
